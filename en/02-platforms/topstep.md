# TopStep

## Overview

TopStep (formerly TopstepTrader) is a US-based futures proprietary trading firm founded in Chicago in 2012 by Michael Patak. It is widely credited as the **inventor of the Trading Combine model** — the evaluation-based prop funding structure that has since been replicated by dozens of prop firms globally. TopStep focuses exclusively on futures markets (not forex or CFDs), uses CME Group instruments, and partners with major futures data and execution infrastructure. As of 2026, TopStep has paid out over $102 million to funded traders.

---

## History and Founding

**Michael Patak** is a former Dow futures floor trader at the Chicago Board of Trade (CBOT). He founded TopstepTrader in July 2012 on the CBOT trading floor, modeling the evaluation process on the NFL Scouting Combine — hence the name "Trading Combine."

Key milestones:

- **July 2012**: TopstepTrader launched by Michael Patak; headquartered at 141 W Jackson Blvd, Chicago (steps from CME Group)
- **2012**: Trading Combine model introduced — the first evaluation-based funded futures account program in the industry
- **2016–2018**: Rapid growth; becomes the dominant US futures prop firm
- **2020**: Company rebrands from "TopstepTrader" to **"Topstep"**
- **2021**: Adds TradingView as a supported platform; significant platform diversification
- **2022**: Adds Quantower as a supported free platform
- **2023**: Topstep remains the market leader in futures prop trading; competitor firms (Apex, Earn2Trade, Uprofit) emerge using similar models
- **July 7, 2025**: All new Trading Combines transition exclusively to **TopstepX** (Topstep's proprietary platform powered by ProjectX technology)
- **August 2024**: Default Daily Loss Limit removed from Trading Combines on TopstepX (retained on other connected platforms)
- **January 2026**: New profit split of **90/10** for traders joining on or after January 12, 2026
- **2026**: 102M+ in cumulative payouts to traders; 13,600+ Trustpilot reviews at 4.3 stars

## The Trading Combine Model

TopStep invented the "Trading Combine" — the industry template for evaluation-based prop funding:

### How It Works
1. **Purchase a Combine**: Trader pays a subscription fee ($50–$150+/month depending on account size)
2. **Pass the evaluation**: Trade a simulated futures account to prove profitability while staying within defined risk limits
3. **Get funded**: Upon passing, trader receives access to a live funded account
4. **Trade and earn**: Trader keeps 90% (post-2026) of approved payouts

### Combine Parameters (as of 2025–2026)

| Account Size | Profit Target | Max Loss Limit | Daily Loss |
|---|---|---|---|
| $50K | $3,000 | $2,000 | Removed (TopstepX) |
| $100K | $6,000 | $3,000 | Removed (TopstepX) |
| $150K | $9,000 | $4,500 | Removed (TopstepX) |

Note: On third-party platforms (NinjaTrader, Quantower, TradingView, Tradovate), the Daily Loss Limit objective remains enforced. The Daily Loss Limit removal applies only to the TopstepX platform.

### Key Rules
- **Maximum Loss Limit (MLL)**: Total account drawdown cannot exceed the threshold; hitting MLL ends the Combine
- **No time limit**: Traders can take as long as needed to pass (unlike FTMO's 30-day limit)
- **Consistency**: No specific consistency rule (unlike some competitors), but unusually large winning days can be flagged
- **Instruments**: Limited to CME Group futures (ES, NQ, YM, RTY, CL, GC, ZB, etc.)
- **No forex or CFDs**: Topstep is exclusively a futures firm

## Platforms

TopStep officially supports multiple trading platforms:

### TopstepX (Primary as of July 2025)

Topstep's proprietary platform built on **ProjectX** technology:
- CME market data updated every 50 milliseconds
- Risk rules (max loss, daily lock-out) enforced directly in platform
- Required for all new Trading Combines starting July 7, 2025
- Free to use during Combine and Funded stages
- Web-based and desktop

### NinjaTrader 8

- The original and most popular platform for Topstep traders
- Connects via Rithmic data feed
- Advanced charting, NinjaScript strategies, Level II DOM
- Available for Funded accounts; fee applies at funded stage
- Remains the preferred choice for experienced futures traders on Topstep

### Quantower

- Free professional trading platform
- Supports advanced order flow tools (footprint charts, volume profile)
- Added by Topstep approximately 2022
- Connects via Rithmic

### TradingView

- Cloud-based charting; widely used by retail traders
- Topstep added TradingView integration approximately 2021
- Limited execution capabilities compared to NinjaTrader or Quantower
- Suitable for chart-based traders who don't need order flow tools

### Tradovate

- Web-based futures trading platform
- Added as a supported option
- Simpler execution interface; suitable for discretionary traders

### Rithmic Data Feed

All third-party platforms (NinjaTrader, Quantower, Tradovate) connect to Topstep via **Rithmic** — the industry-standard futures data and routing infrastructure. Rithmic provides:
- Real-time CME Globex tick data
- Account management API (Topstep uses this to monitor drawdowns in real-time)
- Order routing to exchanges

## Instruments

TopStep supports **CME Group instruments only**:

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| Equity Index | ES (S&P 500), NQ (Nasdaq 100), YM (Dow), RTY (Russell 2000) |
| Energy | CL (Crude Oil), NG (Natural Gas) |
| Metals | GC (Gold), SI (Silver) |
| Interest Rates | ZB (30Y T-Bond), ZN (10Y T-Note), ZF (5Y) |
| FX Futures | 6E (EUR/USD), 6J (JPY/USD), etc. |
| Micros | MES, MNQ, MYM, M2K (micro versions of above) |

No forex (spot), no CFDs, no crypto. This is a defining differentiator from CFD-based prop firms like FTMO, which target forex and CFD traders.

## Business Model

TopStep's revenue comes primarily from:

1. **Combine subscriptions**: $50–$150+/month fees paid by evaluation traders
2. **Account resets**: Traders who fail can reset their Combine for a fee
3. **Live trading infrastructure**: Fees charged when funded traders trade (platform fees, data fees)
4. **Reset fees**: Funded traders who breach risk limits can "reset" for a fee

The majority of evaluation traders fail to pass — this is how the business model is sustainable. Only profitable, risk-controlled traders ultimately receive funded accounts.

## Distinctions vs CFD/Forex Prop Firms

TopStep is structurally different from FTMO, FundedNext, or The5ers:

| Dimension | TopStep | FTMO / Forex Props |
|---|---|---|
| Instruments | CME futures only | Forex, indices, commodities, crypto via CFD |
| Regulation | CFTC-adjacent (futures market) | Less regulated (CFD/retail forex) |
| Platform ecosystem | NinjaTrader, Quantower, TopstepX | MT4, MT5, cTrader |
| Country of origin | USA (Chicago) | Europe (Prague), Israel, Dubai |
| Business model | Same evaluation model | Same evaluation model |
| Time limits | None | Typically 30 days (FTMO) |
| Liquidity | Real CME market liquidity | Synthetic/interbank CFD |
