# The5ers

## Overview

The5ers (stylized "The 5%ers") is an Israeli proprietary trading firm founded in 2016. It funds forex, indices, commodities, and crypto traders through an evaluation-based model with a stated mission to fund the top 5% of traders — hence the name. The5ers distinguishes itself through longevity (one of the older prop firms), a high Trustpilot rating (4.8/5 from 21,000+ reviews), multiple funding program options including an instant-funding path, and a conservative, trader-centric approach to risk rules. As of 2025, it supports MT5 and cTrader; MT4 is no longer offered.

**Founding year**: 2016  
**Headquarters**: Israel (Tel Aviv area)  
**Status**: Privately held; no public ownership or shareholder disclosure

The5ers was founded in Israel in 2016, making it one of the older prop firms in the industry. The company pre-dates the explosion of CFD prop firms that followed FTMO's commercialization of the challenge model. The5ers operates as a fully remote company with an international client base.

Key milestones:

- **2025**: cTrader added as a supported platform alongside MT5; MT4 dropped
- **2025–2026**: Over 30,000 verified payouts; accounts scalable to $4 million; strong reputation in the industry maintained

## Funding Programs

The5ers offers three distinct program structures — each targeting a different trader profile:

### 1. High Stakes

Challenge-based evaluation designed for serious discretionary traders:
- **How it works**: Pass a one-step or two-step challenge with defined profit targets and risk limits
- **Starting capital**: Up to $100,000 simulated
- **Scalability**: Can grow to $4,000,000 as performance milestones are hit
- **Profit split**: 50%–80% (rises with each scaling step)
- **Time limit**: Unlimited (no deadline pressure)
- **Target trader**: Patient, risk-controlled trader focused on long-term account growth

### 2. Hyper Growth (Instant Funding)

Immediate funded account without an evaluation challenge:
- **Cost**: $260–$850 (one-time fee depending on account size)
- **How it works**: Pay the fee, receive funded account immediately — no challenge required
- **Profit split**: Up to 100%
- **Starting capital**: Lower than High Stakes; scales as performance is demonstrated
- **Target trader**: Confident, experienced trader who wants immediate live trading without an evaluation phase
- **Key distinction**: Rare in the prop firm industry; most competitors require evaluation; instant funding is a significant differentiator

### 3. Bootcamp

Structured educational and trading program:
- Begins with lower-funded account
- Systematic trading curriculum alongside live account trading
- Designed for developing traders who want structure and education alongside funding
- Least common of the three programs by market demand

## Risk Rules and Trading Conditions

The5ers applies a relatively trader-friendly ruleset:

- **Daily loss limit**: Applies; typically 4–5% of account balance
- **Maximum drawdown**: Static; typically 8–10% depending on program
- **No time limit**: Traders have unlimited time to pass evaluations in High Stakes
- **Leverage**: Conservative — typically 1:10 to 1:30 depending on instrument (intentionally low to discourage overtrading)
- **Scaling mechanism**: Account size increases as profit milestones are reached without drawdown breaches
- **Withdrawals**: Bi-weekly (every 14 days); minimum $150; payment via bank transfer (3% fee), Wise/crypto (2% fee), or Hub Credits (0% fee via internal credit system)

### Prohibited Strategies

- HFT (high-frequency trading)
- Rollover arbitrage
- Spread arbitrage
- Tick scalping
- Strategies exploiting demo environment pricing inefficiencies

Standard scalping, swing trading, algorithmic strategies (via MT5/cTrader AutoTrade), and weekend holds are permitted.

## Scaling Model

The5ers' scaling model is a key product feature:

- Funded traders who demonstrate consistent profitability receive account size increases
- Maximum account size: $4,000,000 (as of 2025)
- Scaling is performance-based; no additional fees to scale
- Profit split percentage increases with each scaling tier

This model is designed to incentivize long-term trading relationships rather than one-time challenge completions.

## "Low-Leverage Instant Funding" Distinction

The5ers' Hyper Growth instant funding at conservative leverage (1:10 in some configurations) represents a distinct positioning:

- Most instant-funding prop firms use higher leverage to allow traders more room to hit targets
- The5ers' conservative leverage is intentional: targets traders who manage risk through position sizing rather than leverage
- The low-leverage Hyper Growth option is specifically noted as a differentiator in comparison pieces (per PropFirmsReview.com and similar aggregators)
- This aligns with The5ers' brand as a "serious" prop firm targeting professional-quality traders
